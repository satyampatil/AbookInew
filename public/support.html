About Abooki

Abooki is an experimental platform designed to explore the intersection of Artificial Intelligence and creative writing. It serves as a dynamic library where users can generate, read, and share AI-crafted stories instantaneously.

The goal isn't just to generate text, but to create a cohesive reading experience—complete with simulated book covers, reading modes, and a community-driven library where AI creations live alongside human curation.
